Sigma User Guide

Introduction

Welcome to Sigma, the app that helps you achieve track your progress in the gym! This user guide will walk you through the various features and functionalities of Sigma so that you can get the most out of the app.

Your Home Page

Tracking Workouts

Sigma makes it easy to track your workouts.

  1. Simply start a new workout by pressing the "START NEW WORKOUT" button to start a new workout

  2. You can then enter a title for your workout
  3. Then enter an exercise name and then click the add exercise button
  4. Then click the add set button. Repeat for as many sets you want in an exercise
  5. Fill out your sets with the reps and weight from your set
  6. Once your workout is finished press the end workout button (WARNING: Workout will not be saved unless you press this button)
  7. You can then view your completed workout
  8. You can then return home by pressing the "Home" button

Your Workout Page

Your workout page has a timer that records the length of your workout and a stop watch you can manually reset and use at your convenience (such as tracking rest time between sets)

  1. Here you can see the timer
  2. Here you can see your stopwatch
  3. Click this button to reset the stopwatch

Viewing Personal Records

  1. Your Squat, Bench and Deadlift are automatically tracker here (when adding them as an exercise in a workout they must be spelt exactly like this: Squat, Bench, Deadlift
  2. You can search for an exercise here to view your current PR and previous
  3. Enter the exercise name here (it must match exactly how you enter it in a workout)
  4. Click the "Search" button to search your entered PR
  5. Your PRs will appear here

Storing Workouts

On your home page your workouts are stored either in folders created by you or just simply on your home screen

  1. To create a new folder press the "ADD FOLDER" and fill out the form on your screen
  2. Enter the name of your new folder
  3. Click the "OK" button to create the folder
  4. The folder is now visible on your home page
  5. To move a workout to a folder click this button then enter the name of the folder in the pop up box
  6. Then open your folder to view the workouts within

NOTE: to move a workout to the home page simply move the workout to "Home"

NOTE: if you move a workout to a folder that does not exist a new folder will be created with the name of the folder you enetered

Conclusion

Thank you for using Sigma. We hope this user guide has been helpful in getting you started with the app. If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Happy training!
